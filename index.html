OUR STORY we are hip pop
FOUNDERS: EMMA & KENNY
It all started in 2018, we built a brewery in Emma’s garage - and began selling our homebrew at farmers markets across Manchester and Cheshire. Fast forward 4 years, 1 brewery, 2 rebrands, 1 Kickstarter and over 1 million bottles later and the Hip Pop team now brew award-winning Kombucha in a busy craft brewery near Manchester. In 2019 we ran a Kickstarter campaign and were completely bowled over by your generosity and support. Every day we feel grateful for the amazing Hip Pop community who get behind the brand and help to push us forward.
